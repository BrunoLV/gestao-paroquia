# Gestão Paróquia

Gestão Paróquia is a monolithic application designed for the NS Fatima parish to provide a robust, reliable, and secure platform for efficient management of parish affairs.

## Vision

The project aims to centralize administrative themes, starting with calendars and schedules, and expanding to encompass the full range of parish management needs.

## Key Features

- **Event Management**: Complete lifecycle of parish events, including recurrence and approval workflows.
- **Location Management**: Management of parish spaces, including capacity and characteristics.
- **IAM & Governance**: Advanced management of Users, Roles, and Organizations with delegated administration.
- **Project Tracking**: Association of events with specific parish projects and aggregation of status/metrics.
- **Member Management**: Management of parish members, pastorals, and movements.
- **Observability**: Comprehensive tracking of operations and system metrics.

## Technical Foundation

Built with **Java 21** and **Spring Boot 3**, the application follows **DDD (Domain-Driven Design)** and **Clean Architecture** principles to ensure long-term maintainability and extensibility.
